Trust & Safety

Your safety isn't a feature —
it's the foundation.

ID Verification
Every person on HaiK is verified.
Real names. Real faces. Real accountability.

All users verify their identity through secure methods. No fake profiles, no bots. The people you meet are exactly who they say they are.

✦
Live Check-Ins
Someone always knows where you are.
If you want them to.

Optional live location sharing during meetups means a trusted friend or family member can follow along. Always your choice, always your control.

✦
Gentle Accountability
We don't punish. We nudge.
Because people deserve kindness, not shame.

Small emotional reminders — not hard rules — encourage people to show up, be present, and respect each other's time. Connection starts with care.

✦
Review-less Design
We are not here to rate people.
We're here to understand them.

No public star ratings. No scorecards. Just private feedback that quietly improves how we match people — without turning anyone into a product.

✦
Invisible Mode
You can always disappear.
No questions asked. No pressure.

Pause your visibility whenever you need to. Browse freely, take a break, recharge — others simply won't see you're available. Your presence is always opt-in.

✦
AI Tone Matching
Before you even say hello,
we've already broken the ice.

Our system quietly matches compatible communication styles — introverts with introverts, high-energy with high-energy — so first moments feel natural, not forced.
